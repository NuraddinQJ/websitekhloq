Maintaining the Three Essential Documents

Proof of identity documents form the backbone of your stay in Russia. Take care of the safety of your passport and immigration paper,

Keep them in a safe place and always check their validity and renew them before they expire.

Issuing Residency Upon Arrival

Applying for residency on the first day of arrival in Russia is not just a routine procedure, it is mandatory. Make sure to arrange your appointment with local authorities to ensure legal residency during your study period.

It is extremely important for students to register their residency in Russia immediately upon arrival. This registration process ensures legal residence in the country and facilitates access to services and government support.

Certificate Equivalency for Students

For students wishing to pursue their specializations after the preparatory year, starting the certificate equivalency process in January is necessary as the equivalency could take between 45 to 60 days. Starting early allows more time for any adjustments or corrections that may be necessary.

For medical students wishing to study specialization after the preparatory year, it is advised to begin the certificate equivalency procedures in January, as it takes between 45 to 60 days. Completing this step early ensures avoiding any potential delays that may affect the study path.

Living in University Housing

University housing provides a golden opportunity to integrate with other students and practice the language on a daily basis. This choice helps to accelerate the adaptation process to the new environment and enhances learning opportunities.

We recommend that students stay in university housing to enhance interaction with other students and practice the Russian language daily, which aids in quick integration and improving language skills.

Timely Visa Renewal

It is important to renew your visa at least 45 days before its expiration date. Delay in renewal can lead to legal issues and may risk your ability to stay in the country.
